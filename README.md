# 🏡 Airbnb Pricing Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **interactive Tableau dashboard built on the Airbnb 2016 dataset** to analyze how **location, bedroom count, and seasonal demand trends** influence listing prices and revenue.

The objective was to transform raw Airbnb listing data into a **decision-ready business intelligence dashboard** that helps uncover pricing opportunities and high-performing neighborhoods.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Airbnb hosts and short-term rental businesses often struggle with:

* setting the right price for different property sizes
* identifying high-value zipcodes
* understanding how revenue changes across the year
* spotting neighborhoods with stronger earning potential

This dashboard solves that by providing **interactive pricing, geo, and trend insights**.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1) Average Price by Bedroom

* Analyzes how pricing changes from 1 to 6 bedrooms
* Highlights the strong relationship between property size and average price

### 2) Listing Distribution by Bedroom

* Shows the count of listings by bedroom type
* Helps identify market supply distribution

### 3) Zipcode Price Benchmarking

* Compares average prices across zipcodes
* Identifies premium and underpriced areas

### 4) Geo Map Insights

* Interactive Tableau map for location-based pricing analysis
* Makes neighborhood trends easy to interpret visually

### 5) Yearly Revenue Trend

* Displays revenue progression over time
* Helps identify seasonality and demand patterns

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Location is one of the strongest pricing drivers**
* Listings with more bedrooms consistently achieve higher average prices
* Some zipcodes show significantly higher revenue potential
* Revenue trends reveal clear seasonal demand fluctuations
* Smaller properties dominate listing volume, while larger ones drive higher price points
